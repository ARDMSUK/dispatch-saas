# CABAI Platform - Tenant Onboarding Guide

Welcome to CABAI! This guide is designed to help you, the fleet owner, set up your dispatch system from scratch. Don't worry—you don't need to be a technical expert to get things running smoothly.

---

## Step 1: Initial Login and Password Change
When your account is created, you will receive a temporary password.
1. Go to your unique login URL.
2. Enter your email and the temporary password.
3. The system will immediately prompt you to change your password for security reasons. Pick a strong, memorable password.

## Step 2: Configure Your Profile and Branding
Make the system yours!
1. Navigate to **Settings** (the gear icon on the left).
2. Go to the **Organization** tab.
3. Enter your official company name, support phone number, and support email.
4. Upload your company logo and select your primary brand color. These will appear on the dispatch console and in customer emails.

## Step 3: Add Your Vehicles
Before you can dispatch jobs, you need vehicles.
1. Click on **Vehicles** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ Add Vehicle**.
3. Enter the license plate (registration), make, model, and select the vehicle type (e.g., Saloon, MPV, Executive).

## Step 4: Add Your Drivers
1. Click on **Team & Drivers** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ Add Driver**.
3. Enter their basic details (Name, Phone Number, Callsign).
4. Assign them the vehicle you created in Step 3.
5. Set their **Commission Rate** if applicable (e.g., 20% platform fee vs 80% driver take-home).

## Step 5: Understand the Dispatch Console (The Main Screen)
The Dispatch Console is where the magic happens. Here is what the colored badges mean:
- 🟠 **PENDING:** A booking was created (by the AI or Web Booker) but you have not manually accepted it yet. **Click the 3 dots and select "Accept & Confirm" to officially lock it in and notify the customer.**
- 🔵 **CONFIRMED (Unassigned):** The booking is accepted, but no driver has been dispatched yet.
- 🟣 **RES (Reserved):** A driver is reserved for the job, but hasn't officially started heading there yet.
- 🟢 **DISPATCHED:** The job has been sent to the driver's app.

## Step 6: Connect Payments (Optional but Recommended)
To take card payments directly from passengers:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing & Payments**.
2. Connect your Stripe, SumUp, or Zettle account.
3. Once connected, passengers can pay via secure links, and the funds will route directly into your business bank account.
