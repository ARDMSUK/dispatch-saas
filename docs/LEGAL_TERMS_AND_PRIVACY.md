## PRIVACY POLICY & GDPR COMPLIANCE

**1. DATA PROCESSOR VS DATA CONTROLLER**
Under GDPR, the Fleet Operator (Tenant) acts as the **Data Controller** for all passenger information (names, phone numbers, addresses). The CABAI Platform acts solely as the **Data Processor**.

**2. DATA WE COLLECT ON YOUR BEHALF**
To facilitate dispatch operations, we process:
- Passenger names, phone numbers, and email addresses.
- GPS coordinates for pickup and drop-off locations.
- Real-time location data for active drivers (if live tracking is enabled).
- Anonymized chat transcripts processed via OpenAI for booking intent.

**3. DATA RETENTION & DELETION**
Passenger data is stored securely in our database. As the Data Controller, you may request the deletion or anonymization of passenger records at any time. We automatically purge raw AI chat transcripts after 30 days unless required for dispute resolution.

**4. THIRD-PARTY SUB-PROCESSORS**
To provide the service, data may be securely transmitted to:
- **Neon/AWS:** Database hosting.
- **OpenAI:** Processing conversational text to extract booking details (Data is NOT used to train OpenAI models).
- **Twilio/Resend:** Transmitting SMS and Email confirmations.
- **Stripe/SumUp:** Securely processing financial transactions (CABAI does not store raw credit card numbers).

By using the platform, you explicitly consent to the use of these sub-processors.
